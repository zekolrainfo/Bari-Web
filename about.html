Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Quality

Bari Print was founded in 2010 in the heart of Melapalayam with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, affordable printing and publishing services to the local community.

What started as a small typing and printing shop has grown into a full-service creative print studio offering everything from custom stickers and book binding to Arabic manuscript work and personalized gift items.

Over the years, we have earned the trust of schools, businesses, mosques, government offices, and individuals — becoming the most reliable print partner in the region.

Our Journey

Milestones Over the Years

Key moments that shaped Bari Print into what it is today.

2010

The Beginning

Bari Print opens its doors in Melapalayam as a small typing and photocopying center serving the local community.

2013

Expanded to Full Printing

Invested in commercial printing machines, adding offset and digital print services for businesses and institutions.

2016

Arabic Book Specialization

Launched dedicated Arabic book and Quran binding services, serving mosques and Islamic institutions across the region.

2019

Custom Gifts & Gifts Division

Added personalized gift items, trophies, acrylic work, and rubber stamps to cater to growing demand.

2024

15 Years Strong

Celebrating over a decade of service with 5000+ satisfied customers and a full suite of 20+ print services.
